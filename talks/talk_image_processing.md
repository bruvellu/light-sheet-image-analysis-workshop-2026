### Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

- Understand bioimage analysis workflows and pipelines
- Acknowledging the characteristics of bioimage analysis and why it is needed
- An overview of classical bioimage analysis pipelines and a examples of classical algorithms
- Apply denoising and background subtraction techniques
- Perform segmentation and labeling on microscopy images
- Use classical image processing algorithms in Fiji and other bioimage tools

:::
