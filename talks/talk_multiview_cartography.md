## Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

- Understand multiview image registration and fusion strategies
- Explain how multiview imaging improves resolution and reduces artifacts
- Apply tissue cartography concepts to map anatomical structures
- Design multiview experiments for optimal resolution coverage
