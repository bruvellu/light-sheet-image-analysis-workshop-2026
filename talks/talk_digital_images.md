### Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:

- Explain fundamental concepts of digital image representation (pixels, voxels, bit depth)
- Understand how digital images represent information
- Be aware of how light intensity information is digitalized
- Bridge concepts between optical and digital resolution
